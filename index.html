Our Story

Cooking with heart & soul

Tucked away at the back of Blackrock Market, Ciamei is a cosy Italian gem run by Claudio Moscati — a passionate cook who brings the flavours of Italy to Dublin every single day.

What started as a small café has grown into one of Blackrock's most loved restaurants, known for wood-fired thin-crust pizza, handmade pasta, and dishes made from the freshest ingredients with real Italian care.

The atmosphere is warm, the portions are generous, and Claudio's hospitality makes every guest feel like family. Whether you're popping in for a lunchtime bite or settling in for a Saturday night dinner — you're always welcome.

— Claudio & the Ciamei family
